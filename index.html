The Methodology

Our study leverages Intel Power Gadget to measure the precise energy footprint (in Joules) of various hybrid sorting implementations across massive datasets.

Dual Pivot Quicksort

The standard in modern Java and Android systems. High performance with a focus on cache locality.

Timsort

Derived from merge sort and insertion sort. Designed to perform exceptionally well on real-world data.

Introsort

A robust hybrid of Quicksort, Heapsort, and Insertion sort, ensuring O(n log n) even in worst-case scenarios.

Recombinant Sort

Experimental hybrid approach merging multiple strategies for optimal energy-to-runtime ratio.

Hybrid Quick-Insertion

Combines the speed of Quicksort with the efficiency of Insertion Sort for small subarrays.
