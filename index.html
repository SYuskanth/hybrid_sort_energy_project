Analyzing the Impact of Hybrid Sorting Algorithms on Energy Usage

This research evaluates the energy efficiency and execution time of various sorting strategies across massive datasets using Intel Power Gadget. A study dedicated to the field of sustainable software engineering.

Research Overview

With the increasing demand for energy-efficient software, this research evaluates the performance of hybrid sorting algorithms under controlled experimental conditions. The results provide valuable insights into selecting optimal algorithms for energy-aware computing.

Python Intel Power Gadget Pandas NumPy

Objectives

  • Evaluate energy consumption
  • Analyze input size impact
  • Integer vs. Floating point
  • Time vs. Energy relationship

Dataset & Methodology

The dataset includes information such as algorithm type, input size, input distribution, execution time, energy consumption, and data type (integer and floating-point). Measurements were logged with high-resolution sampling for accurate reporting.
